Chemical Composition

The formulation utilizes four key reagents to ensure stability, efficacy, and safety.

Active Agent

Isopropyl Alcohol

Conc: 99.8% (Final: 75%)

The primary antimicrobial agent. It kills 99.9% of germs by dissolving lipid membranes and denaturing proteins.

Humectant

Glycerol

Conc: 98% (Final: 1.45%)

Added to retain moisture in the skin. It counteracts the drying effect of alcohol, preventing dermatitis.

Sporicide

Hydrogen Peroxide

Conc: 3% (Final: 0.125%)

Inactivates contaminating bacterial spores in the solution and the storage bottle. Not for hand disinfection.

Diluent

Distilled Water

Type: Sterile Cold

Used to dilute the alcohol to the optimal concentration range (60-80%) where it is most biologically active.
