Stage 1: Input

Collection and precise measurement of raw reagents (Alcohol, H2O2, Glycerol) based on stoichiometric calculations.

Stage 2: Processing

Controlled mixing in a sterile vessel. The order of addition is critical to prevent adverse reactions and ensure solubility.

Stage 3: Output

Transfer to dispensing units and mandatory 72-hour quarantine to ensure the final product is spore-free.
